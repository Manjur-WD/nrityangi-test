About Us

Our Odissi Dance School is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Odissi dance. With a passion for this classical Indian dance form, we offer comprehensive training programs for dancers of all ages and levels. Our experienced and qualified faculty is committed to providing a nurturing and supportive learning environment where students can develop their skills, explore their creativity, and appreciate the cultural significance of Odissi.

Experienced Faculty:

Our team of experienced Odissi dancers and teachers brings years of expertise and knowledge to our programs.

Comprehensive Training:

We offer a wide range of classes, workshops, and performances to cater to dancers of all levels, from beginners to advanced.

Authentic Odissi:

Our curriculum is rooted in the traditional techniques and styles of Odissi, ensuring that students receive authentic training.

Cultural Immersion:

We foster a sense of cultural appreciation and understanding through our programs, helping students connect with the rich history and traditions of Odissi.

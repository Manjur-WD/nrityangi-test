Welcome to Nrityangi Dance Academy

Nrityangi Dance Academy is dedicated to the preservation and promotion of the ancient Indian classical dance form, Odissi. For over 10 years, we have nurtured students of all ages, inspiring them to express their creativity through graceful movements and storytelling.

Our Mission

At the heart of our organization lies a profound commitment to preserving and promoting the exquisite art of Odissi dance. We strive to create an environment that not only nurtures the technical and artistic skills of our students but also fosters a deep sense of community, discipline, and cultural pride. Through our teachings, we aim to instill in our students an appreciation for this ancient dance form, encouraging them to connect with their heritage and share it with others.

Our Vision

Our vision is to emerge as a leading institution for Odissi dance, recognized for our dedication to excellence and innovation. We aspire to inspire students from diverse backgrounds around the globe, encouraging them to delve into and celebrate the rich tapestry of Indian classical dance. By offering exceptional training and creating opportunities for performance and collaboration, we hope to ignite a passion for Odissi that transcends borders, cultivating a global community of dancers who cherish and uphold this timeless art form.

Our Teaching Philosophy

At Nrityangi, we believe in cultivating both technique and expression. Our students are trained in the rich traditions of Odissi while encouraged to express their individuality through dance.

  • Emphasis on Tradition: We ensure that every student masters the foundational techniques of Odissi.
  • Individual Expression: Students are encouraged to explore and express their unique creativity.
  • Holistic Development: We focus not only on dance techniques but also on building confidence and stage presence.
  • Cultural Immersion: Each class is a journey into the rich cultural history of Odisha and Odissi dance.
